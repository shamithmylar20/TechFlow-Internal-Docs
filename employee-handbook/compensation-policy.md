# Compensation & Benefits Policy

## Salary Ranges by Department

### Engineering Department
- Junior Developer: $75,000 - $95,000
- Senior Developer: $95,000 - $125,000  
- Lead Engineer: $125,000 - $150,000
- Engineering Manager: $140,000 - $180,000

### Sales Department
- Sales Rep: $50,000 + commission
- Senior Sales Rep: $65,000 + commission
- Sales Manager: $85,000 + $120,000

- Health Insurance: Company pays 80%
- 401k: Company match up to 6%
